Financial Aid

You can qualify for financial aid if you are signed up for 6 or more credits.

Typical tuition cost after aid is 18k

FAFSA(Free Application for Federal Student Aid) is financial aid that helps pay for college. The Aplication starts in the Winter and gives you money to pay for tuition each semester.

 

While there are lots of scholarships to help with cost, most scholarships are CTE based at Oregon Tech.
